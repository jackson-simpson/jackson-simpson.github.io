My Learning Plan

Introduction

My learning plan will be used to guide my learning during my time at DevAcademy, as well as to review and reflect on when I may be experiencing difficulty with the content.

My Long Term Goals

My goals after DevAcademy is to move into a programming job, either as a web developer or Full-Stack Engineer, using the basis of what I have learnt as a foundation for more learning.

My Learning Strengths and Limitations

I am a persistant learner, someone who is determined to learn, and is curious. I enjoy problem-solving, and the feeling of success when I achieve, which in turn drives my learning forward.

However, I can get easily distracted, I can lose track of time, become fixated on specific tasks; all of which can lead me to fall behind; which eventually leads me to feel discouraged.

Human Skills I would Like to Develop

I would like to develop my interpersonal skills, and try to become less awkward in professional settings.

My Commitments

I will commit to manage my workload. I will do so by:

  • Planning my weeks ahead on the Monday, assigning time based on the given time boxes for the given task for that week.
  • Have an open mindset when working with others.
  • Be patient with myself and others I am working with.
  • Apply techniques like self-timing, and timeboxing to help prevent myself from falling behind.

I will commit to seeking help, by communicating with fellow students when I become stuck for more than 20 minutes. If I am unable to find the answer, I will ask the Facilitators for assistance. I will commit to this, so I am continuing to meet expectations around time-management.

I commit to accounting for important appointments, personal tasks and breaks into my learning plan so that these are structured into my learning.

My Expectations for the Facilitators

My expectations from the Facilitation team are that they are present to answer questions or offer guidance during reasonable hours. I expect the facilitation team to be patient and kind when dealing with myself or any other student.
